About LIVIN

As a skater company, we are dedicated to making the life long dreams of skaters come true BUT a skater cannot succeed without having some style. LIVIN dedicates itself to creating new flexible like clothing, shoes, and accessories that all skaters can wear as a casual outfit or something to skate in. We see to it that every skater cruise around with their swag on.
